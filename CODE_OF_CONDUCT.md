# Code of Conduct

DraftRelay contributors are expected to make participation respectful, safe, and productive.

Examples of positive behavior include using welcoming language, accepting constructive feedback, focusing on what is best for users, and showing empathy toward other contributors. Harassment, personal attacks, discriminatory language, deliberate intimidation, and publishing another person's private information are unacceptable.

Project maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions and participation that violate these expectations. Serious or repeated violations may result in a temporary or permanent ban from project spaces.

Report conduct concerns privately through the repository's monitored maintainer contact. Until a dedicated conduct address is published, use [the private reporting form](https://github.com/raashy/draftrelay/security/advisories/new) and identify the report as a conduct concern. Do not include it in a public issue. Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled as confidentially as practical.

This policy is adapted from the Contributor Covenant, version 2.1.
